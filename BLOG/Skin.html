I-I'm sorry

I wish I wasn't who I am.

I wish I could shed my skin and transform into someone else,

leaving all of my problems and issues on that scaly, dry, old skin.

The skin never peels off or flakes off, but it clings to me.

Under this old, dry, and dirty skin, the skin

that is so healthy and almost glowing is lost.

My emotions erupt like blisters that are desperately

attempting to pull the skin away,

but it just grows back over.

Treatments and essential oils frighten me.

Imagine if I burn.

What if the top layers of my skin rip off,

exposing my flawless underlayers,

which would then bleed endlessly?

What if this flawless hidden skin stings

and recoils when exposed to the air,

forcing it to be covered up with bandages?

What if there is no way to remove my skin and

I am destined to have this old, dry, dirty skin forever?
